Maisons du Monde is a decoration and furnishing brand, for all styles. It is a public limited company, created by Xavier Marie, in 1990. Its head office is located in Vertou, in Loire-Atlantique. It recorded 780 million euros in turnover in 2017.

Maisons du Monde offers many decorative and furnishing items in these stores, in seven different styles: vintage, seaside, classic chic, modern design, country house, industrial and exotic. There are also children's, outdoor and seasonal items. Within the stores, the products are staged by creating inspiration boards.
